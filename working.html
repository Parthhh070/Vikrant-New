Dual-Mode Control System

Vikrant offers exceptional versatility with its dual-mode control system, providing users with the flexibility to choose between manual operation and autonomous navigation. In manual mode, users can control the boat using a Bluetooth connection through a dedicated mobile app, offering precise handling of the boat to tackle specific areas with minimal effort.

Whether it's maneuvering around obstacles or addressing localized pollution, manual control ensures that Vikrant can be guided efficiently by the operator. On the other hand, autonomous mode enables Vikrant to follow pre-programmed GPS waypoints, navigating the lake automatically by calculating its position in real-time. This allows the boat to cover larger areas without constant human supervision, ideal for large-scale cleaning operations where hands-on control would be impractical.
