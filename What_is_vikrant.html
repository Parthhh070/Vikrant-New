Why is vikrant made?

Water pollution is a growing concern across the globe. Lakes, rivers, and water bodies are increasingly suffering from the accumulation of waste, including plastic, water hyacinths, and other pollutants. This has adverse effects on aquatic life, water quality, and overall environmental health.

  • Over 8 million tons of plastic waste end up in the oceans each year.
  • Water hyacinth growth disrupts aquatic ecosystems, reducing water oxygen levels.
  • Manual cleaning methods are labor-intensive, inefficient, and costly.

Join us in this mission as we strive to make a positive impact on aquatic ecosystems, providing intelligent solutions for a better, cleaner future. It's worth noting that our smart boat "Vikrant" is not only a technological marvel but also features patented technology, ensuring that our innovation stands out in the field.

What is Vikrant?

Welcome to our innovative journey with the smart boat "Vikrant." We are committed to revolutionizing water cleaning processes with state-of-the-art technology.

The smart boat, equipped with advanced IoT sensors, is designed to efficiently tackle floating waste and water hyacinth in lakes and water bodies. Our commitment lies in creating a sustainable and cleaner environment.

Together, we can contribute to restoring the health of aquatic ecosystems. By integrating cutting-edge technology into our smart boat, "Vikrant," we are addressing the growing problem of water pollution with efficiency and precision. With these innovations, we are paving the way for a cleaner, greener future, setting new standards in environmental sustainability.
